# Cold email sequence

The app is only half the play — how you present it is everything. Two emails.

## Email 1 — the offer (no link yet)

Goal: get a "yes". You offer to *build something custom* for them. You're not
faking it — the app makes the promise real; it just takes minutes instead of weeks.

**Subject:** `Should I build this for you?`

**Body (EN):**
```
Hi [First name],

I noticed you're running [detected tech / observed signal] at [Company].

I built a tool that creates [custom deliverable] specifically for your setup.
Would it be OK if I spent some time and put together [a few automations / a
backlink profile / a teardown] you could use in one click?

Just say yes and I'll get started.

[Your name]
```

**Body (RU — KORA voice):**
```
Здравствуйте, [Имя]!

Заметил, что у вас на [Компания] используется [замеченный сигнал / стек].

Я собрал инструмент, который делает [конкретный результат] под ваш случай.
Будет нормально, если я потрачу немного времени и подготовлю для вас
[несколько сценариев / карту / разбор], который можно применить в один клик?

Просто ответьте «да» — и я начну.

[Имя]
```

## Email 2 — the delivery (after they reply yes)

Send the app link with their URL/company **prefilled** so they land straight on a
result built for them.

**Body (EN):**
```
Here you go — I put together [specific deliverable] for [Company]:

[link to the app, prefilled: https://app.example.com/?url=THEIR-URL]

You can [import it in one click / download it]. Let me know what you think —
happy to hop on a quick call.

[Your name]
```

**Body (RU):**
```
Готово — вот [конкретный результат] для [Компания]:

[ссылка на приложение с предзаполнением: https://app.example.com/?url=THEIR-URL]

Можно [импортировать в один клик / скачать]. Дайте знать, как вам —
с радостью созвонюсь на пару минут.

[Имя]
```

## Who this works best for

- Decision makers at SMBs (10–200 employees) with a real, tangible problem.
- Or audiences with "shiny object syndrome" who love new tools/tech.
- Notably strong in previously "impossible" niches: agencies selling to agencies,
  AI companies selling AI services — because the app proves you're not just
  another cold email.

## Honesty guardrails

- Never state a reply-rate or result as a guarantee to the lead.
- Personalization signals in Email 1 must be real (actually detected), not invented.
- Follow KORA content-integrity rules for all Russian client-facing copy.
